Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to Automatic Companions?

To reach our headquarters, you'll have to go north on Main Street, before turning left on Cog Avenue, and then, taking a right at Circuit Boulevard. Once on Circuit, drive about 2,000 feet and we'll be on your right.

Do the robots need to be taught anything?

By default, our bots are programmed with knowledge on how to perform a set of tasks specific to their model. However, they may require guidance so that they can accomplish their tasks to your liking, and if their User Manual doesn't list a specific task you'd like them to peform, they'll have to be taught how to do it. Don't worry, though. They're quick learners!

Are the robots good around kids?

Of course! Each bot, no matter the model, is designed to be child-friendly, with a knack for befriending, looking after, and even protecting children, should the need arise.

Do you accept returns?

Robots purchased brand-new can be returned up until six months after the date of purchase. And upon returning your bot, they'll be labeled "Pre-Owned", and put back on sale for 30% off the standard, retail price. Bots bought pre-owned cannot be returned.
